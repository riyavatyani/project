WELCOME TO THE SOUTHERN PART OF INDIA

South India, according to combined definition by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Geological Survey of India and Ministry of Culture refers to the southern region of India comprising the states of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu and the Union Territories of Puduchery and Lakshadeep .

Some of the major highlights of southern India are the magical destinations of Bengalaru, Hyderabad( charminar) and KanyaKumari (Cape Comorin). The region, bordered by on the way by the arabian sea on the east by the Bay of Bengal on the north by the Vindhya and Satpura Range.

A journey to southern India promises a showcase of some of the country’s finest cultural and architectural sights.South India boasts of having two enchanting styles of rock architecture, the pure Dravida style of Tamil Nadu and the Vesara style (also called Karnata Dravida style) present in Karnataka .

An expedition to southern India wouldn’t be complete without a tour of Bengalaru or the Hampi, its cultural heritage under the Nizam of Hyderabad. "God's own country", famous for spices, backwaters, Ayurveda and the Malabar coast. .
