Popular Places To Visit:

India Gate,Delhi

War Memorial and Vast Public Park

For those wanting to add some extra flair to their visit to this tourist site, look no further than their enchanting light & sound show during evenings.

Gateway of India,Mumbai

Iconic Landmark

Ideal place to enjoy cool sea breeze, watch the city skyline light up as evening sets in, feed pigeons, click fun photos and catch a good meal at one of the nearby eateries.

Golden Temple,Amritsar

Holiest Sikh Shrine

Take a dip in the holy waters of Amrit Sarovar, pay obeisance to Granth Sahib at the main temple and seek divine bliss as you see the peaceful rituals and ceremonies.

Mysore Palace,Mysore

Historical Royal Palace

The palace houses an impressive collection of royal antiques and artefacts. The exterior looks especially striking during Dussehra.And is illuminated with lamps.

Statue of Unity,Vadodara

World's Tallest Statue

You can walk up to the chest level of the statue and enjoy splendid views, explore a variety of cacti at the adjoining Cactus Garden and enjoy the light and sound show here.

Trimbakeshwar Temple

An Ancient Pilgrimage Site

Offer prayers to Lord Shiva, witness the grand Rudra Abhishek ceremony and admire the stunning deities and lingams housed within the temple.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

The Andaman & Nicobar Islands are a group of scenic islands nestled in the eastern part of Bay of Bengal. They are one of the seven Union Territories.

Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach is one of the most famous attractions in Havelock Island and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands itself. Commonly known as Beach No. 7, Radhanagar has been given the title of the best beach in Asia and the 7th best beach in the world by Time Magazine in 2004.

Cellular Jail

This three-storeyed prison, constructed by Britishers in 1906, is a pilgrimage destination for freedom fighters. This colossal edifice has mutely witnessed the most treacherous of inhumane atrocities borne by the convicts, who were mostly freedom fighters.

Elephant Beach

With its brilliant coral reefs and alluring cyan waters, the Elephant beach is one of the locations that add to the beach paradise reputation of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. These waters are a prime destination for tourists wishing to engage in water sports.

Lakshwadeep Island

Lakshadweep is a tropical archipelago of 36 atolls and coral reefs in the Laccadive Sea, off the coast of Kerala, India.

Minicoy Island

The island, also called Miliku, is one of the most prominent parts of the Lakshadweep islands. The island is the central hub for all plans to Lakshadeep, or it’s sister islands.

Kavaratti Island

The white sand and beautiful sunsets make this island one of the best places to see in Lakshadweep. This peaceful lagoon has a surprising amount of greenery and plantations. Kavaratti is the ideal getaway for nature lovers.

Marine Museum

This is where one can witness some of the wealthiest showcases of marine life and artifacts. The museum is established with an aim to educate the masses about the importance of aquatic life and show the various species of fish available.
